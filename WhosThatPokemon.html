WhosThatPokemon Help

Can you guess Who's That Pokémon?

whosthatpokemon

Description: Guess Who's that Pokémon in 30 seconds!

You can optionally specify generation from gen1 to gen8 only.

Example: - [p]whosthatpokemon - This will start a new Generation. - [p]whosthatpokemon gen1 - This will pick any pokemon from generation 1 for you to guess.

Arguments: - [generation] - Where you choose any generation from gen 1 to gen 9.

Usage: ,whosthatpokemon
