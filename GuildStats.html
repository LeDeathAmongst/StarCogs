GuildStats Help

A cog to generate images with messages and voice stats, for members, roles, guilds, categories, text channels, voice channels and activities!

guildstats

Description: Generate images with messages and voice stats, for members, roles, guilds, categories, text channels, voice channels and activities.

Usage: ,guildstats

guildstats guild

Description: Display stats for this guild.

Usage: ,guildstats guild

guildstats ignoreuser

Description: Ignore or unignore a specific user.

Usage: ,guildstats ignoreuser

guildstats messages

Description: Display stats for the messages in this guild.

Usage: ,guildstats messages

guildstats category

Description: Display stats for a specified category.

Usage: ,guildstats category

guildstats voice

Description: Display stats for the voice in this guild.

Usage: ,guildstats voice

guildstats toggleactivitiesstats

Description: Enable or disable activities stats.

Usage: ,guildstats toggleactivitiesstats

guildstats top

Description: Display top stats for voice/messages members/channels.

Usage: ,guildstats top

guildstats enable

Description: Enable the cog in this guild/server.

Usage: ,guildstats enable

guildstats ignorechannel

Description: Ignore or unignore a specific channel.

Usage: ,guildstats ignorechannel

guildstats ignoreactivity

Description: Ignore or unignore a specific activity.

Usage: ,guildstats ignoreactivity

guildstats ignorecategory

Description: Ignore or unignore a specific category.

Usage: ,guildstats ignorecategory

guildstats channel

Description: Display stats for a specified channel.

Usage: ,guildstats channel

guildstats graphic

Description: Display graphic for members, roles guilds, text channels, voice channels and activities.

Usage: ,guildstats graphic

guildstats activities

Description: Display stats for activities in this guild.

Usage: ,guildstats activities

guildstats activity

Description: Display stats for a specific activity in this guild.

Usage: ,guildstats activity

guildstats disable

Description: Disable the cog in this guild/server.

Usage: ,guildstats disable

guildstats ignoreme

Description: Asking GuildStats to ignore your actions.

Usage: ,guildstats ignoreme

guildstats role

Description: Display stats for a specified role.

Usage: ,guildstats role

guildstats member

Description: Display stats for a specified member.

Usage: ,guildstats member

guildstats setdefaultstate

Description: Enable or disable by default the cog in the bot guilds.

Usage: ,guildstats setdefaultstate
