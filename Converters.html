Converters Help

Some converters.

conv

Description: Some utility converters.

Usage: ,conv

conv todate

Description: Convert a unix timestamp to a readable datetime.

Usage: ,conv todate

conv fahrenheit

Description: Convert Fahrenheit degree to Celsius or Kelvin. See correct usage bellow.

Usage: To Celsius: [p]conv fahrenheit celsius To Kelvin: [p]conv fahrenheit kelvin (You can also use [p]conv f c or [p]conv f k)

Usage: ,conv fahrenheit

conv fahrenheit celsius

Description: Convert Fahrenheit degree to Celsius.

Usage: ,conv fahrenheit celsius

conv fahrenheit kelvin

Description: Convert Fahrenheit degree to Kelvin.

Usage: ,conv fahrenheit kelvin

conv lb

Usage: [p]conv lb kg

Usage: ,conv lb

conv tounix

Description: Convert a date to a unix timestamp.

Note: Need to respect this pattern %Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S. Year-month-day Hour:minute:second Minimum to work is Year.

Usage: ,conv tounix
