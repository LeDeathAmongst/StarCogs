Highlight Help

Slack-like feature to be notified based on specific words.

highlight

Description: Slack-like feature to be notified based on specific words outside of at-mentions.

Usage: ,highlight

highlight list

Description: List your highlighted words for the current guild.

Usage: ,highlight list

highlight timeout

Description: Set the timeout between consecutive highlight triggers.

This applies to consecutive highlights within the same channel. If your words are triggered within this timeout period, you will only be notified once.

Parameters:

seconds: int The timeout between consecutive triggers within a channel, in seconds. Minimum timeout is 0 (always trigger). Maximum timeout is 3600 seconds (1 hour).

Usage: ,highlight timeout

highlight ignore

Description: Ignore certain words to avoid having them trigger your DMs.

Suppose you have a word X in your highlighted words, and a word Y you are ignoring. Then, we have some scenarios as below: - "X something something": triggers DM. - "X Y": does NOT triggers DM. - "X something something Y": does NOT trigger DM.

Usage: ,highlight ignore

highlight ignore del

Description: Remove an ignored word from the list.

Usage: ,highlight ignore del

highlight ignore add

Description: Add words to your ignore list.

Parameters:

word: str The word you wish to ignore.

Usage: ,highlight ignore add

highlight ignore list

Description: List ignored words.

Usage: ,highlight ignore list

highlight guild

Description: Guild-wide settings

Usage: ,highlight guild

highlight guild channel

Description: Channel denylist.

Channels on this list will NOT trigger user highlights.

Usage: ,highlight guild channel

highlight guild channel del

Description: Remove a channel from the denylist.

Parameters:

channel: discord.TextChannel The channel you wish to remove from the denylist.

Usage: ,highlight guild channel del

highlight guild channel add

Description: Add a channel to the denylist.

Channels in this list will NOT trigger user highlights.

Parameters:

channel: discord.TextChannel The channel you wish to not trigger user highlights for.

Usage: ,highlight guild channel add

highlight guild channel show

Description: List the channels in the denylist.

Usage: ,highlight guild channel show

highlight channelDeny

Description: Stops certain channels from triggering your highlight words

Usage: ,highlight channelDeny

highlight channelDeny list

Description: Sends a DM with all of the channels you've stopped from triggering your highlights

Usage: ,highlight channelDeny list

highlight channelDeny remove

Description: Remove a channel that was on the denylist and allow the channel to trigger highlights again

Parameters:

channel: discord.TextChannel The channel you wish to receive highlights from again.

Usage: ,highlight channelDeny remove

highlight channelDeny add

Description: Add a channel to be blocked from triggering highlights

Parameters:

channel: discord.TextChannel The channel you wish to block highlight triggers from.

Usage: ,highlight channelDeny add

highlight del

Description: Remove a highlighted word in the current guild.

Usage: ,highlight del

highlight blacklist

Description: Blacklist certain users from triggering your words.

Usage: ,highlight blacklist

highlight blacklist clear

Description: Clear your user blacklist.

Will ask for confirmation.

Usage: ,highlight blacklist clear

highlight blacklist list

Description: List the users on your blacklist.

Usage: ,highlight blacklist list

highlight blacklist add

Description: Add a user to your blacklist.

Parameters:

user: discord.Member The user you wish to block from triggering your highlight words.

Usage: ,highlight blacklist add

highlight blacklist del

Description: Remove a user from your blacklist.

Parameters:

user: discord.Member The user you wish to remove from your blacklist.

Usage: ,highlight blacklist del

highlight add

Description: Add a word to be highlighted in the current guild.

Usage: ,highlight add
