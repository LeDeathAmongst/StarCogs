Badges Help

Create fun fake badges based on your discord profile

badges

Description: Creates a fun fake badge based on your discord profile

badge is the name of the badges do [p]listbadges to see available badges

Usage: ,badges

gbadges

Description: Creates a fun fake gif badge based on your discord profile this only works if you have a gif avatar

badge is the name of the badges do [p]listbadges to see available badges

Usage: ,gbadges

listbadges

Description: List the available badges that can be created

Usage: ,listbadges
