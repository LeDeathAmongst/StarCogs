VoiceLog Help

Logs users joining and leaving a VC, inside the VC chat itself.

voicelog

Description: Voice Log configuration

Usage: ,voicelog

voicelog disable

Description: Disable voice log for the whole guild.

Usage: ,voicelog disable

voicelog enable

Description: Enable voice log for the whole guild.

Usage: ,voicelog enable
