### unbset

**Description:** Manage various settings for Unbelievaboat.

**Usage:** `<@1275521742961508432>unbset`

**Description:** Set the min or max payout for working or crimes.

**Usage:** `<@1275521742961508432>unbset payout`

### unbset cooldown

**Description:** Set the cooldown for the work, crime or rob commands. Minimum cooldown is 30 seconds.

The time can be formatted as so `1h30m` etc. Valid times are hours, minutes and seconds.

**Usage:** `<@1275521742961508432>unbset cooldown`

### roulette

**Description:** Bet on the roulette wheel.

**Current supported bets**:
Single   - Any single number.
Colors   - Red/Black
Halfs    - 1st/2nd half
Even Odd - Even or Odd
Dozens   - 1st/2nd/3rd Dozen (Groups of 12)
Colums   - 1st/2nd/3rd Column.
- This is based on the English version of the roulette wheel.

**Usage:** `<@1275521742961508432>roulette`
