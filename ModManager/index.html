ModManager Help

Force ban/kick users so that they stay in the ban/kick list even if someone tries to manually unban them.

modmanager

Description: Mod Manager Commands.

Usage: ,modmanager

modmanager clear

Description: Clear the ban or kick list.

Usage: ,modmanager clear

modmanager list

Description: Showcase the ban or kick list.

Usage: ,modmanager list

modmanager reason

Description: Change the ban or kick reason.

Usage: ,modmanager reason

modmanager modlog

Description: Toggle the Mod Manager modlogs.

Usage: ,modmanager modlog

modmanager settings

Description: Show the Mod Manager settings.

Usage: ,modmanager settings

modmanager toggle

Description: Toggle Mod Manager.

Usage: ,modmanager toggle

modmanager force

Description: Force add users to the ban or kick list.

Usage: ,modmanager force

modmanager force ban

Description: Force add users to the ban list.

Usage: ,modmanager force ban

modmanager force kick

Description: Force add users to the kick list.

Usage: ,modmanager force kick
